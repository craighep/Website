From small beginnings... to all things code

I have held a great amount of curiosity for all things code since I was 11, when I found a little program called Scratch. From this, I went on to make a couple of games with this software with my friends, and in doing so, won a competition in the council area where I was awarded a game programming day with a Rockstar games developer. Ever since that day I've been hooked, and followed this path to where I am today.

And I like a bit of web development, too.

Although I study a degree scheme that is primarily focused on Software development and Software engineering, I am also extremely passionate about web development. Not only have I created a range of websites for myself, but I have also created multiple websites for my friends, done for free of course. I do this both because I gain experience and because of my pure enjoyment out of doing so. One of the main reasons for me being here is to code.
